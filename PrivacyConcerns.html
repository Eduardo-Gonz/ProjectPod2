Privacy Concerns

Online dating, for many, is a new world that comes with privacy questions and concerns. Here we provide our members with responses to common privacy related questions.

  • How private are my accounts, the information I put into my accounts, and my photos?

    • Each time you login there is new encryption code so it makes it hard for people to hack into your account. Only information and photos you allow to be public will be viewed by other individuals. In addition to this, there are multiple private settings that you can set on your account in order to reduce the chance of receiving unwanted attention from unpreferred individuals. We ask that you keep private information such as workplace and personal account information to yourself in order to prevent unwanted predicaments.

  • How can I keep my privacy protected?

    • Avoid revealing your address, place of work, and other locations you wish to keep private. Our servers will not ask members for their location, so it is important to keep this information limited and disconnected from your accounts. In addition to your location, we ask our members to limit the amount of social media connections they make with other platforms, meaning please do not feel forced to connect your Facebook, Instagram, or other social media account to your Lovepoint account as this is not a requirement for our members.

  • How long will my account stay on the platform?

    • Depending on the membership plan you obtain the status of your account will vary, however if you choose to cancel your membership your account will then be deleted in order to prevent unwanted situations. This process will take 3-5 business days, but following this timeline your account should no longer be displayed on our platform.
